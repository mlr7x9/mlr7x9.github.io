SoundCloud SDK Retrieving an audio file

For this part of my exploration I've decided to look at the SoundCloud SDK (https://developers.soundcloud.com/docs/api/sdks#javascript) and integrating SoundCloud into my site to listen to music. I explored examples like this one http://connect.soundcloud.com/examples/basic.html. I've also added a streaming button that only pulls a song, not the visuals. An interesting thing about this functionality, is that it will continue to load the song every time the button is pushed. So if the button is pushed 15 times, the song will play over itself 15 times.

SoundCloud Widget Streaming some audio via a widget

Then I took it a step further and integrated streaming of a specified playlist or song into my site http://connect.soundcloud.com/examples/streaming.html. The information about the widget can be found here https://developers.soundcloud.com/docs/api/sdks#javascript.
